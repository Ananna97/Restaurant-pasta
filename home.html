Il Mondo della Pasta.. soul food...


A warm atmosphere on the hills of beautiful Campina and great pasta recipes that will make you belive that you are in Toscana. Tortellini, spaghetti, bucatini, lasagna and a lot of others homemade pasta covered in tasty sauces, served in a steam of savour. In summer, you eat on the terrace with a view of the forest and the mountains at the horizon. seasonal ingredients.
